# backend
Remaimber-it is a personal learning companion designed to help you learn, retain, and master key concepts across any subject.
Create your own question bank, answer freely in your own words, and let an AI evaluate your responses to guide your progress.

You can build custom training sessions — timed challenges, topic-specific quizzes, or fully personalized learning modes — all tailored to how you want to study.

Empower your learning through feedback, repetition, and smart reminders.
